About

From executive support to predictive modelling.

I started my career keeping an executive's calendar and building campaign trackers by hand. Since then I've moved through sales operations, business analysis, and now freelance data science — always drawn back to the same question: what does the data actually tell us to do next?

I'm currently expanding into machine learning, generative AI, and cloud analytics, applying that same reporting discipline to predictive modelling — from credit risk to healthcare outcomes.
